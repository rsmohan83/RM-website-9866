Human Capital


It refers to the stock of people posses different skills, availability, efficiency to produce various goods and services that helps to promote country GDP(Growth and Developmemt). 

         If large no. of skillful people add into existing productive population then, country's efficiency for the promotion of Growth and Development tends to rise and a country become a progressive nation where GDP growth constantly moves into upward direction.


Gross National Product


It is a sum total of factor income earn by normal or non-residents of company during an accounting year including net factors income from abroad
